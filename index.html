Sudoku Teacher Privacy Policy

最后更新：2026 年 7 月 21 日

简而言之：「数独教室」不收集、不上传、不分享您的任何个人信息。所有数据都只保存在您自己的设备上。

感谢您使用「数独教室」（以下简称「本应用」）。我们非常重视您的隐私。本隐私政策说明本应用如何处理与您相关的信息。

1. 我们收集哪些信息

本应用不收集、不上传任何个人身份信息。本应用没有账号系统，无需注册或登录即可使用全部功能。

本应用运行过程中产生的以下数据，全部仅保存在您的设备本地，不会传输到我们或任何第三方的服务器：

  • 做题进度与记录：您正在进行或已完成的数独题目状态、完成时间、已完成数量等，用于恢复进度和显示「进行中」「已完成」标记。
  • 应用偏好设置：例如是否已进行过评价等状态标记。

上述数据保存在设备的本地存储中。如果您删除本应用，这些数据也会随之删除。

2. 相机权限的使用

本应用提供「拍照识别数独题目」功能。当您主动使用该功能时，系统会请求相机权限。

拍摄的照片仅在您的设备本地进行文字识别处理，用于将题目自动填入棋盘。照片和识别结果不会被上传、保存或分享。识别完成后照片即被丢弃。

如果您不使用拍照识别功能，可以拒绝相机权限，这不影响本应用的其他功能。您可以随时在系统「设置」中管理相机权限。

3. 内购与打赏

本应用可能提供可选的打赏或内购项目。所有交易均通过 Apple App Store 的应用内购买（StoreKit）完成，由 Apple 处理付款。

我们不会接触、收集或存储您的支付信息（如银行卡号、Apple ID 密码等）。相关交易信息的处理适用 Apple 的隐私政策。

4. 网络与第三方

本应用的核心功能完全在本地运行，不需要联网。本应用：

  • 不包含第三方广告；
  • 不包含第三方数据分析或追踪工具（SDK）；
  • 不向任何第三方分享您的数据。

5. 儿童隐私

本应用适合各年龄段用户，包括儿童。由于本应用不收集任何个人信息，因此不存在收集儿童个人信息的情况。我们建议家长陪同低龄儿童使用电子设备。

6. 您的权利

由于本应用的所有数据都保存在您的设备本地，您对这些数据拥有完全的控制权：

  • 您可以随时通过应用内操作清除或覆盖做题进度；
  • 您可以随时在系统「设置」中撤销相机权限；
  • 您可以随时删除本应用，届时所有本地数据将一并删除。

7. 政策的变更

如本应用未来的功能发生变化，我们可能会更新本隐私政策，并在本页面公布更新后的版本与日期。建议您不定期查看本页面以了解最新内容。

Sudoku Teacher · Privacy Policy

数独教室 隐私政策

Last updated: July 21, 2026

In short: Sudoku Teacher does not collect, upload, or share any of your personal information. All data stays only on your own device.

Thank you for using Sudoku Teacher (the "App"). We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information related to you.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect or upload any personally identifiable information. The App has no account system; all features are available without registration or sign-in.

The following data generated while using the App is stored only locally on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party:

  • Puzzle progress and records: the state of puzzles you are working on or have completed, completion time, and number solved — used to restore your progress and show "in progress" / "completed" markers.
  • App preferences: such as whether you have already been prompted to leave a review.

This data is kept in your device's local storage. If you delete the App, this data is deleted along with it.

2. Use of the Camera

The App offers a "scan a Sudoku puzzle with the camera" feature. When you actively use this feature, the system will request camera permission.

Photos you take are processed for text recognition entirely on your device, solely to fill the puzzle into the board automatically. Photos and recognition results are never uploaded, saved, or shared. The photo is discarded once recognition is complete.

If you do not use the scan feature, you may decline camera permission without affecting other features. You can manage camera permission at any time in the system Settings.

3. In-App Purchases & Tips

The App may offer optional tips or in-app purchases. All transactions are processed through Apple App Store In-App Purchase (StoreKit), with payment handled by Apple.

We do not access, collect, or store your payment information (such as card numbers or Apple ID password). Apple's handling of transaction data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

4. Network & Third Parties

The App's core features run entirely offline and require no internet connection. The App:

  • contains no third-party advertising;
  • contains no third-party analytics or tracking tools (SDKs);
  • does not share your data with any third party.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages, including children. Because the App collects no personal information, no personal information about children is collected. We recommend that parents supervise young children's use of electronic devices.

6. Your Rights

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it:

  • You can clear or overwrite your puzzle progress at any time within the App;
  • You can revoke camera permission at any time in the system Settings;
  • You can delete the App at any time, which removes all local data.

7. Changes to This Policy

If the App's features change in the future, we may update this Privacy Policy and post the revised version and date on this page. We recommend reviewing this page periodically.
